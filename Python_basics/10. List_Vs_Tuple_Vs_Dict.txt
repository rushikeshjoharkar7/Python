
Differences between list, tuple and dictionary

List : [v1,v2,v3] , Indexing , Ordered , Mutable , Duplicates accepted

Tuple : (v1,v2,v3) , Indexing , Ordered , Immutable , Duplicates accepted

Set : {v1,v2,v3} , No Indexing , Random ordered , Mutable , Duplicates ignored

Dictionary : {k1:v1, k2:v2} , No Indexing , Ordered , Mutable , Keys are unique and Values can be duplicated
